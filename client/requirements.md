## Packages
recharts | For visualizing pedestrian vs green time data
framer-motion | For smooth animations of traffic lights and transitions
lucide-react | For beautiful iconography

## Notes
- The app uses a dark/futuristic aesthetic suitable for an AI/simulation tool.
- Simulation logic is performed on the backend via `/api/simulation/calculate`.
- Real-time graph data is fetched from `/api/simulation/graph-data`.
- Historical runs are stored and displayed.
